Lumielle Account Deletion

This page explains how users can request deletion of their Lumielle account and related personal data.

App: Lumielle
Support Email: elivy_help@daum.net

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

elivy_help@daum.net

Subject: Lumielle Account Deletion Request

Please include:

  • Your Lumielle login email
  • Your login provider (Google/Kakao)
  • Your deletion request

Deleted Data

  • Account profile information
  • User preferences
  • Saved/liked content information
  • Associated app usage data

Retained Data

Purchase and transaction records may be retained temporarily if required by law, payment processing, fraud prevention, or tax/accounting obligations.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are normally processed within 7 days.
