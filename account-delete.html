Lumielle Account Deletion

This page explains how users can request deletion of their Lumielle account and related personal data.

App: Lumielle

Support Email: elivy_help@daum.net

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to elivy_help@daum.net with the subject Lumielle Account Deletion Request.

  • Your Lumielle login email
  • Your login provider, such as Google or Kakao
  • A clear account deletion request

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile information
  • User preferences
  • Saved or liked content records
  • Associated app usage data

Data That May Be Retained

Purchase and transaction records may be retained temporarily where required by law, payment processing, fraud prevention, tax, accounting, or dispute resolution obligations.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are normally processed within 7 days after verification.
